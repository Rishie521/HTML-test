    Learners License

    Being a good driver is about more than handling a car. You also need to know what road signs mean, what makes for good road etiquette and how to anticipate dangers before they happen. This is why the learners test is designed, to help keep you and others safe. Use our learner’s classes and online test to pass your learners license the first time.

    Tips to pass your licence

    Here is a list of tips to follow to help you to pass your drives and your learners test.

    • Make sure you understand the procedure of what is going to happen on the test day.
    • Book a mock test near to your test date.
    • Ask your driving instructor of what will be expected of you during the day.
    • Do not think of your driving test while you are on the test.
    • Arrive early so that you can review the yard test to prepare yourself for the day.
    • Take a day off a week before your actual test date so that you can watch other students that will be doing their tests.
